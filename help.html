What is To-Do List?

A to-do list is the list of tasks/things that you have to do. To Track the tasks effectively and to remember the tasks effectively the to do list plays an important role.It can help to prioritize your work and get the work done.Make the To do list improves the organizing mind.

Why You need a To Do application?

The major problem in the human beigns is that they can't do multi-tasking and the end up in confussion .So, it is important for every person to make the sorting of the works that they have.Here,Comes the todo application that will remind the tasks that they wants to do in the time .

How to Handle the app?

Adding the task:

The app consist of the input text box in the place below the To-do list heanding is the main part and here is the place where you type your task.In that box type your task and to send the task press enter to add the task.

Done Button:

If the you complete the task press the done button to strike the task and stay on the list untill deleting the task.

Delete Button:

The delete button will delete the task that is in the list .Untill you press the delete button the task will stay in the list.
